Gulash Recipe

Recipe Guide


Take 500g of Beef (you can add pork if you like) ,a big pott, 2 onions, 3-4tomatoes, tomatosauce, hot chilli (if you like) and green paprika. forst cook the meat for at least 90h, after 20min add the onions and after another 10mins add a cup of Water. Then after 90mins add all cut ingredients and cook for another 45min. season with salt and pepper after that serve (with creme fraiche if you like).

  • 500g Beef (mixed with pork is optional)
  • 3-4 Tomatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • Chillipepper (optional)
  • Green Paprika
  • 1 Cup of Water

steps

  1. coook meat in a big pott for at least 90min
  2. after 20mins add onions
  3. after another 10min add cup of Water
  4. after the 90min add all ofter cutted ingredients
  5. let it cook for another 45min
  6. season with salt and pepper and serve
