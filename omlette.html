Omlette Recipe

Recipe Guide

Take 2-3 eggs and mix them good, add to that tomatoes or red paprika or onions. season with salt & pepper and hot chili (if you like). you can mix a little bit of cheese to it but DONT add milk. fry everything in a pan until its golden brown.


ingridients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1-2 Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Onions
  • Chillipepper (optional)
  • red Paprika
  • cheese

steps

  1. mix eggs
  2. add Tomatoes and optional ingridients
  3. season with salt&pepper
  4. fry for 5-10 min until golden brown
